About VIESTA 2019

VJC's open house 2019 had the theme VIESTA. The entire open house was built on the idea of having a carnival-style open house, where all Victorians, regardless of CCAs came together to showcase their talents to the public. The fun carnival-style open house aimed to showcase the Victorian sprit. In line with the theme of VIESTA, VRMC and Science Society had a collaboration.
