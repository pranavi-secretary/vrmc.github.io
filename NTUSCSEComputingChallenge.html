About NTU SCSE Computing Challenge 2020

The competition hosted by the school of computer science and engineering in NTU requires students to create a mobile or web application and host it on Amazon Web Services. Two teams from VJC took part in the competition.

Team 1:


Their project:


The team created an application to help students track their mood. The application also allows students to reflect on their overall mood and set goals to improve thier mood. This application allow students to figure out what makes them feel upset, and allows them to correct these actions, so that they can live happier and more content lives.


Team 2:


Their project:


The team created an application to help students track their spending. The application allows students to plan thier expenditure, as well as control the amount of money they spend each month. This app allows students to differentiate beween "needs" and "wants" and allows them to cut down on unnecessary spending, and save up to buy more lecture notes.

VJC Teams:

Both teams worked hard over the December holidays to prepare for the competition, and they learned a lot from this experience.
