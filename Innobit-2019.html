About InnoBit Chalenge 2019

Organised by the Leng Kee Youth Executive Committee, this challenge builds upon students' creativity to come up with technologically-powered solutions to take on societal issues. This is achieved using the Micro:Bit, a computer that can easily integrate controller buttons, sensors, LED display and many other devices. It can even be remotely programmed right from your smartphone! Using their creativity and some coding, our team consisting of Yuki, Xavier, Rubesh, Anthony and Jwalin, came up with a scheduled pill dispenser to improve medication adherence in the elderly, by automatically dispensing pils at the stipulated time intervals. This would serve as a reminder to the elderly and simultaneously ensures that the correct dosage is taken.
