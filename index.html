What do we develop?

VEX Robotics

We have been specialised in VEX since the advent of this Club, and have worked in various projects involving Advanced Mechanics and Automation.

Drones

We work on Fixed-Wing Planes, FPV drones and quadcopter.

Arduino & Electronics

We work on student initiated projects which are developed with Arduino, Electronics and Raspberry Pi.

Programming

We equip members with programming skills, and teach them useful programming languages like Python. We are the latest addition to the VRMC family.
