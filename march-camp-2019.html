VRMC March Camp 2019

Our march camp serves as a platform for the seniors to bond with the incoming J1 cohort and equip them with the basic skills needed for the various specialisations. It is a two-day event from 9am to 5pm during the March Holidays that incorporates various group-based activities facilitated by the J2s, as well as workshops conducted by external vendors such as SUTD.
